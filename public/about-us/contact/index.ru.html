The best way to get in touch with us is to fill out a help ticket. Please visit user.riseup.net to create a ticket.

To contact us regarding general questions about our collective, send mail to collective@riseup.net. Any help questions directed to this email will be ignored.

Our mailing address is:

Riseup collective members and friends are often found on irc.indymedia.org in the #riseup chat room. You can use the web interface at chat.indymedia.org to contact us there if “irc” is not a familiar acronym.
